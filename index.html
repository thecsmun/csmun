🎯 Welcome to CSMUN 2026!

Your complete guide to navigating the website

1Navigation Bar Overview

The navigation bar at the top contains all essential pages:
  • Home – Main landing page with intro and committees overview
  • About – Learn about CSMUN's vision and mission
  • Committees – Explore all 6 committees (UNSC, UNHRC, DISEC, Lok Sabha, IP, BCCI)
  • Team – Meet the Secretariat and Executive Boards
  • Resources – ⭐ Your one-stop hub for all conference materials
  • Guide – Complete MUN guide for delegates

2🏠 Home Page Highlights

  • Countdown Timer – Days until the conference
  • Committees Section – Interactive cards with committee details and agendas
  • Secretary General's Message – Welcome note from the SG
  • Allocation Matrix Link – See your committee and country assignment

3📚 Resources Page – Complete Breakdown

The Resources page is your essential hub. Here's everything you'll find:

📄 Liability & Consent Form

Mandatory for all delegates. Download, print, sign, and submit at registration.

📋 Conference Policies

Complete rules, code of conduct, dress code, and disciplinary guidelines. Read carefully!

📖 Background Guides

Committee-specific research guides available for:

  • UNHRC
  • DISEC
  • BCCI
  • IP Style Guide (for International Press delegates)

🔗 Allocation Matrix

Google Sheet showing all delegate allocations, countries, and committees.

🌐 Research Resources

External links to:

  • UN Official Documents
  • News Sources (BBC, Al Jazeera, Reuters)
  • UN Data & Statistics
  • MUN Research Guides (Best Delegate)
  • Video Tutorials

❓ FAQ Section

Common questions about eligibility, fees, dress code, and more.

4✅ Action Steps for Delegates

  1. Download Liability Form from Resources page
  2. Read Conference Policies thoroughly
  3. Check Allocation Matrix for your committee assignment
  4. Download your committee's Background Guide
  5. Start researching using the Research Resources links
  6. Read the Guide page if you're new to MUN

5📱 Mobile Navigation

On mobile, tap the ☰ hamburger menu (top right) to access all navigation links.

ABOUT CSMUN

Empowering Voices.
Shaping Futures.

CSMUN 2026 brings together young leaders, innovators and changemakers to debate global issues, build diplomacy and create a better world. Through structured committee sessions, delegates engage in meaningful discourse on pressing international challenges.

Deliberate. Decide. Deliver. — This is our commitment to excellence in diplomacy and debate.

A Word from the Secretary General

“Model United Nations asks an important question of every delegate: not simply what you believe, but how you defend it, challenge it, and refine it in the presence of others. At CSMUN 4.0, I hope you embrace every debate with an open mind, every negotiation with purpose, and every disagreement with respect. My message to all the delegates is: If you leave this conference seeing the world through a perspective other than your own, then we will have achieved something far more meaningful than any resolution alone could accomplish.

Let CSMUN 4.0 be remembered not for how loudly we spoke but for how deeply we left an impact!”

Valivarthi Shree Deetya

Secretary General, CSMUN 2026
